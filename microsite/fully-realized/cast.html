Cast Episodes Awards

CHARACTERS & CAST

Wirt

A nervous, poetry-writing teenager who plays the clarinet, overthinks everything, and struggles with confidence.

Elijah Wood

An acclaimed film and voice actor bringing Wirt’s nervous charm to life.

Greg

Wirt’s younger half-brother—cheerful, imaginative, and impulsive.

Collin Dean

Recognized young voice actor who perfectly captures Greg’s character.

Beatrice

A sarcastic, prickly bluebird who travels with the brothers.

Melanie Lynskey

Actress well known in film. Adds warmth and wit to Beatrice’s voice.

The Woodsman

An older, mysterious character wandering the Unknown carrying a lantern and chopping Edelwood trees.

Christopher Lloyd

Legendary actor who lends gravitas and depth to the Woodsman.

The Beast

A mysterious creature who haunts the Unknown, preying on lost souls and turning them into Edelwood trees.

Samuel Ramey

Opera singer with a powerful bass voice, giving the Beast his theatrical presence.

Sara

Wirt’s crush and a fellow high school student back in the real world.

Emily Brundige

Writer and animator with soft voice that makes Sara feel genuine and relatable.

Auntie Whispers

An eerie old woman who lives in a creaky house with her niece, Lorna.

Tim Curry

Beloved stage and screen performer voicing Auntie Whispers with eeriness.

Greg’s Frog

Greg’s unnamed frog, who sings and plays a surprisingly big role in the story.

Jack Jones

Classic crooner whose rich vocals bring nostalgia to Greg’s singing frog.
